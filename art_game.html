How to Play - Art Challenge

  • Look at the Sample Grid on the left — this shows the pattern you need to copy
  • Choose colors from the palette below the current color indicator
  • Click cells in the Your Grid to fill them with your selected color
  • Match the sample grid exactly to get a perfect score
  • You only get one submit, so check your drawing carefully
  • Your score is based on accuracy (70%) and speed (30%). Time penalties begin after 30 seconds.
