How to Play

Welcome to Career Quest!

Choose between exciting career paths and test your skills:

  • Tech Career: Solve JavaScript coding challenges. Read the problem, write your solution in the editor, and run it against test cases.
  • Engineering Career: Build logic circuits! Drag and drop logic gates (AND, OR, XOR, NAND) onto the circuit board and toggle inputs to light the bulb.
  • Art Career: Create pixel art! Use the tools to fill in the grid and match the reference image.
  • Business Career: Make smart decisions! Choose the best option to grow your business and earn profits.
  • Medical Career: Diagnose patients! Analyze symptoms and choose the correct treatment.
  • Customer Service Career: Help customers! Respond to inquiries and solve problems efficiently.

Scoring: Your score (0-100) is based on accuracy (70%) and speed (30%). Earn up to 3 stars!
